## clone:it x Johann-Jakob-Widmann-Schule 📚🏗

A few weeks ago, we had the privilege of presenting our **clone:it app** to the students and teachers of **Johann-Jakob-Widmann-Schule Heilbronn** in **Baden-Württemberg**. It was an inspiring session where we explored the exciting possibilities of **digitalization in the construction industry** and showcased how **BIM models** can unlock even greater potential when combined with **AR technology**—accessible through a smartphone or tablet! 📱✨

## Engaging with Future Construction Professionals

We had a great time **engaging with students** and sparking curiosity about how digital tools are shaping the future of construction. The interactive nature of **BIM and AR technology** made a strong impression, showing students how **real-world projects** can be enhanced through **digital transformation**.

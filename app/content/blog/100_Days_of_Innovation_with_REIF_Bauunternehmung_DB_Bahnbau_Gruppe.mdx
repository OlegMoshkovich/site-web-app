## 100 Days of Developing, Testing, and Innovating 🚀

After an intense **Proof-of-Concept (PoC)** phase with **REIF Bauunternehmung GmbH & Co. KG, DB Bahnbau Gruppe GmbH, and DB mindbox**, we have successfully demonstrated how **bringing BIM models directly to the construction site** delivers real value.

These past months have been dedicated to **refining, improving, and expanding** the core functions of our app—ensuring that it meets the **demands of real-world construction environments**.

## Real-World Impact

Through hands-on testing and collaboration, we have seen first-hand how BIM adoption can drive **efficiency, accuracy, and smarter decision-making** in railway construction projects. Key achievements from this PoC include:

- **Seamless BIM model integration** into ongoing construction workflows
- **Faster execution** with immediate access to real-time model data
- **Improved on-site coordination** between project stakeholders

And this is just the beginning! We are committed to working alongside **Deutsche Bahn stakeholders** to further **push BIM adoption** and develop **even more impactful features** for the field.

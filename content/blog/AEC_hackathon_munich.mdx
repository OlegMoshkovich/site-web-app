## 🚀 Exciting News from clone:it at AEC Hackathon Munich!

Last weekend, we had the incredible opportunity to present a **challenge** at the **AEC Hackathon in Munich**.

A diverse and talented team, including experts in **game development**, took on the challenge we proposed:

1️⃣ **Displaying large BIM models spanning several kilometers** on construction sites using **AR**.  
2️⃣ **Automatically detecting and aligning BIM models** with real-world edges based on preselected reference points.

## 36 Hours of Innovation & Collaboration

In just **36 hours**, the team developed an **innovative app and simulator** to tackle both tasks. Their solution impressed the jury and showcased the immense **potential of AR in the AEC industry**.

We are thrilled to announce that the team **won "Best Startup Potential" at the hackathon!** 🏆
